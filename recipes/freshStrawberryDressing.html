Fresh Strawberry Dressing

Description

Pureed strawberries make a distinctive base for a colorful dressing with a creamy consistency. It is also a nice way to dress a salad of arugula, goat cheese and toasted pecans.

Time: 10 mins

Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries, rinsed, hulled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 3/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons almond oil or canola oil

Steps

  1. Place strawberries, vinegar, pepper, sugar and salt in a blender or food processor.
  2. Process until pureed, stopping once or twice to scrape down the sides.
  3. Add oil and process until smooth.
